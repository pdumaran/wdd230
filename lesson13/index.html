Welcome to Bacolod Chamber of Commerce

Bacolod Chamber of Commerce's purpose is to promote the development of the business interests of individuals and organizations in Bacolod City. It supports its members' interest with relevant city, provincial and national administrations and organizations. It also creates productive networking and business opportunities for all its members, local and abroad. It addresses a wide range of issues that help businesses succeed from programs to publications, trade and business delegations, to advocacy work.

Bacolod Chamber of Commerce is Influential

The Chamber is an effective advocate for members' business interest. Bacolod Chamber of Commerce leaders regularly meet with senior city, provincial and national government officials to discuss issues pertinent to members and their businesses. Additionally, officials and media often come to Bacolod Chamber of Commerce for advice and counsel on a variety of subjects. IN short, Bacolod Chamber of Commerce influences policy.

Five Day Forecast

Credit financing eyed to stir economy

Rep. Francisco Benitez (Neg. Occ., 3rd District) is looking for ways to access capital to help stir the local economy affected by the coronavirus pandemic. He met with Frank Carbon, chief executive officer of Bacolod Chamber of Commerce, Provincial Administrator Rayfrando Diaz II, and representatives from the Department of Trade and Industry, Development Bank of the Philippines, and Land Bank of the Philippines Wednesday night to “discuss ways and means to help jumpstart the local economy direly affected by the pandemic.”
