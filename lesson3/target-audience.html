Target-audience

Phil Weather is for everybody who is looking to find out about the weather locally and abroad. It is for everybody who has a mobile device and wants to check the current weather update. The people who mainly use its service are the ones who are booking holidays to check the weather in a different country. Its service is also used by anyone who wants to go on a fun day out with family and friends.

Two Personas

Families

  • Age: 18 & above
  • Income: $100,000 annually
  • Education: High School & College graduates
  • Devices: PC & Mac, Tablets, and Smartphone

Scenarios

  1. Dad is trying to find the right time to take a dog to the vet. Since he lives in an area that keeps raining, he needs to check on the weather to travel safely.
  2. Mom is trying to buy groceries for the family. Since she lives outside the city where she usually buys her groceries, she checks out the weather for safe travel.
  3. Dad is a scuba diver. The sea is a very dangerous place to be during a storm, so he checks the weather to know whether it is best to go scuba diving or not.
  4. Mom participates in a community service project to take care of homeless people. She regularly checks on the weather to ensure the community service project will push through without any problems.
  5. A family plans to have a vacation in another state. Mom and dad check on the weather update to ensure safety and quality time together as a family.

Businessmen

  • Age: 18 & above
  • Income: $200,000 annually
  • Education: High School & College graduates
  • Devices: PC & Mac, Tablets, and Smartphone

Scenarios

  1. Mary is working in a legal firm. Her boss instructed her to set an appointment with a client. Since the client lives in another city, she checks on the weather to ensure her safety while traveling.
  2. Robert has a fishing business. A sea is a rough place to work. He checks on the weather to ensure the safety of his employees.
  3. Margarita is a real estate agent. She travels to different states all the time to look for property and clients. She checks on the weather to ensure her safety while she travels.
